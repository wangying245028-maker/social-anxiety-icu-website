Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Social Anxiety ICU! We believe in radical simplicity and privacy by design. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. We are committed to providing a low-pressure, private space for your most important connections.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Identifiers

We collect your **Device ID** (Unique Device Identifier) to recognize your device, manage your account, and maintain your connections within your "Safe List." This allows you to use the app without a complex registration process.

2.2 Presence and Content

24-Hour Ephemeral Storage: Your daily photos and thoughts are stored for 24 hours only. We do not store historical photos or historical mood content.

To enable the "Quiet Update" feature, we collect and temporarily store:

  • The photo you capture for your daily status
  • The mood text or "thought" you share with your circle

All photos and thoughts are automatically cleared after 24 hours.

2.3 Operation Data

We collect **User Operation Data** to understand how the app is being used and to improve the user experience. This includes actions such as:

  • Frequency of status updates
  • Usage of the "Silent Poke" feature

3. Minimal Data Collection

We are committed to data minimization. **We do not collect** any other personal information, such as your phone number, email address, physical address, or contacts list. Beyond the Device ID and the ephemeral 24-hour content mentioned above, we do not track or store your personal data.

4. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Service: To show your 24-hour status to your designated safe list
  • Silent Interaction: To deliver "Silent Pokes" between you and your besties
  • Improvement: To analyze operation data and improve the "safe house" experience

5. Data Retention and Protection

  • Content Deletion: All photos and thoughts are permanently deleted from our servers 24 hours after being posted.
  • No History: We do not maintain a history of your past status updates.
  • Encryption: All data is transmitted securely and stored with industry-standard encryption.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to delete your data at any time. Since we only store content for 24 hours, most of your shared content is removed automatically. For any other data requests, please refer to the app settings or disconnect from the service.
