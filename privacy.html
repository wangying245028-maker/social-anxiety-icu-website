Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to 10People! We believe in radical simplicity and privacy by design. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in our minimalist "safety check" and "mind connection" application.

This policy applies to the 10People application and its related services. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

10People operates on an invite-only model. We collect:

  • Device-generated unique identifier (for user identification)
  • Profile information (name, avatar) you choose to share with your 10 connections
  • Invitation links and connection relationships

2.2 Connection Data

Privacy First: Your status updates are ephemeral. They reset at midnight and leave no permanent history.

We temporarily store:

  • Your "light up" status (visible/not visible)
  • Short status messages (under 20 characters) that you share
  • "Nudge" notifications between your 10 connections
  • Widget display preferences

Important: Status data is ephemeral and automatically cleared at midnight. We do not maintain permanent history logs.

2.3 Device Information

To provide widget functionality and notifications, we collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Push notification tokens
  • App version and language preference

2.4 Usage Analytics

We collect minimal, anonymized data to improve the service:

  • Feature usage patterns (e.g., frequency of "light up" actions)
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

3. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Service: Display your status to your 10 connections and vice versa
  • Notifications: Send nudges and status updates via push notifications
  • Widget Functionality: Power the home screen widget ("The Window")
  • Product Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve the experience
  • Security: Prevent abuse and unauthorized access

4. Information Storage and Protection

4.1 Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Server data is encrypted at rest
  • Access to user data is strictly controlled and logged
  • Regular security audits and updates

4.2 Data Retention

In line with our anti-social design philosophy:

  • Status data: Automatically deleted at midnight daily
  • Nudge notifications: Cleared after viewing
  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for up to 1 year

4.3 Data Location

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard cloud providers with strong privacy commitments.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Within Your Circle: Your status is shared with your designated 10 connections only
  • Service Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (hosting, notifications) bound by strict confidentiality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

We will never use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: View your personal information that we store
  • Right of Rectification: Correct your profile information at any time
  • Right of Erasure: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your connection data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Disconnect from 10People at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

10People is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States and other jurisdictions. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Third-Party Services

10People uses the following third-party services:

  • Push Notifications: Apple Push Notification Service (APNS) and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Cloud Storage: Secure cloud infrastructure providers
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage analytics (opt-out available in settings)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements.

10. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications. Continued use of our services after updates indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
