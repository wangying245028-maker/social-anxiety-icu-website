Product Positioning

Social Anxiety ICU

A low-pressure social circle for socially anxious people.

What it is

Social Anxiety ICU is a minimalist private circle where socially anxious users can quietly log one real daily status and view the real status of their closest besties.

Core experience

  • One daily self-log (photo + mood) with no social pressure.
  • A private bestie circle for viewing real daily status.
  • Only one interaction: a single tap poke for silent check-ins.

Design principle

No likes. No comments. No noisy social mechanics. Just quiet presence and lightweight connection.
